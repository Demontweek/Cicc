Welcome to CICC

Welcome to the Calabar International Convention Centre (CICC), a place of luxury, comfort, and elegance. We ensure your guests are treated with the utmost care and respect.

Established in 2015 by Cross River State’s former Governor, His Excellency Senator Liyel Imoke, CICC was designed to provide a world-class event space for both local and international gatherings.

Our Journey

For over a decade, we have maintained excellence in service delivery. Our commitment is to ensure your events are memorable, stress-free, and perfectly managed.
